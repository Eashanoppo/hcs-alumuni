# Post-Deployment SEO Checklist: holy-crescent.com

To achieve and maintain a #1 ranking for "Holy Crescent School", follow these steps immediately after your website is live.

## Phase 1: Search Engine Indexing (High Priority)

### 1. Google Search Console (GSC)
- **Action**: Go to [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console).
- **Step**: Add your property `https://holy-crescent.com`.
- **Verification**: Use the HTML tag or DNS record method provided by Google.
- **Sitemap**: Navigate to "Sitemaps" in the sidebar and submit `https://holy-crescent.com/sitemap.xml`.
- **URL Inspection**: Manually "Request Indexing" for your homepage to speed up the process.

### 2. Bing Webmaster Tools
- **Action**: Go to [Bing Webmaster Tools](https://www.bing.com/webmasters).
- **Step**: Use the "Import from Google Search Console" feature to quickly sync your site.

## Phase 2: Local SEO & Authority

### 3. Google Business Profile (Crucial)
- **Action**: Search for "Holy Crescent School Ashulia" on Google Maps.
- **Step**: If you own the profile, update the **Website** field to `https://holy-crescent.com`.
- **Step**: Ensure the Address, Phone Number, and Primary Category ("School") exactly match what is on the website.
- **Why**: This is the #1 factor for ranking in the "Map Pack" at the top of search results.

### 4. Facebook/Social Media Alignment
- **Action**: Go to the official Holy Crescent School Facebook page.
- **Step**: Update the "About" section to link to `https://holy-crescent.com`.
- **OpenGraph Check**: Use the [Facebook Sharing Debugger](https://developers.facebook.com/tools/debug/) to paste your link and click "Scrape Again". This ensures the correct image and title show up when shared.

## Phase 3: Content & Engagement

### 5. Official Launch Post
- **Action**: Post a link to the new alumni portal on Facebook and LinkedIn.
- **Why**: Social signals and initial traffic from real users tell Google your site is relevant.

### 6. Monitor Search Rankings
- **Check**: Once a week, search for "Holy Crescent School" in an incognito window.
- **Goal**: You should see the site climbing. Typically, it takes 2-4 weeks to stabilize near the top after submitting the sitemap.
